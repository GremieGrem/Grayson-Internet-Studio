Creative Brief

Often times it is difficult to locate the specific artists that you need for a job. It could be website design, making a movie, animation for a television show, artists are required for the job, but looking across all sorts of social media sites trying to find those who fit your budget and have the proper skill set you need is very time consuming and discouraging. As someone who wants to collaborate with other artists, entrepreneurs, and innovators alike, I find myself stuck. There are websites that are out there that showcase portfolios of other artists such as Behance, Art Station, or Fiverr. They allow artists to reach out to their communities and audiences, but do they encourage other artists to reach out to each other, no matter their medium or specific craft? My main goal is to make a website that brings artists from varying backgrounds together, make networking easier, faster, more effecient. I want creatives to feel encouraged and empowered by how easy it is to just reach out to one another, how accessible it is to find voice actors, vfx artists, background artists, musicians, directors, writers, anyone. During their collaboration, it would be encouraged to share feedback to each other, and their community about what sort of efforts their new colleagues have managed to accomplish. Showcase what you've gotten done so far in the project with a timeline, show potential goals you want to reach.
