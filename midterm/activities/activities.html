Blue Lake

The Blue Lake, an ideal area to swim around not just for the scenery but the cooling temperature. A relaxing place to rest up after journeying across Hallownest. If you're lucky, some other pilgrims might be taking refuge here as well.

Colosseum of Fools

Any one is allowed to join the fight and participate in combat. Generous rewards are in store for anyone who manages to win the matches, increasing in value the more you climb up the ladder. If you're not interested in that, you're always allowed to sit and watch from the stadium.

The Nailmasters

Across Hallownest are multiple Nailmasters. They can teach you different fighting techniques, making for a useful exercise activity for the day along with giving you a useful ability during your travels. Some of the Nailmasters are quite talkative so don't be afraid to strike up a conversation. The Nailmaster, Sheo, though is more interested in visual arts so if you're looking to get into some painting, he's your guy.
