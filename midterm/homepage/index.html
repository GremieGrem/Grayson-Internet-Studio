Welcome!

Hallownest, the remnants of an ancient kingdom, ruined by both time and a mysterious infection that ravaged the land. Despite its flaws, Hallownest provides pilgrims with an enriching experience with a variety of fascinating cultures, locations, and stories to uncover. However, pilgrims are advised to bring supplies and a sturdy weapon due to the many dangers of the place.

Factoids

  • Once ruled by the The Pale King and The White Lady
  • An underground kingdom, prepare for the chill
  • Insectoid citizens from pillbugs to spiders
  • Might be haunted by an angry God!
