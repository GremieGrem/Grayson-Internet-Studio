Matpat For President

Bio

Mathew Robert Patrick, also know as 'MatPat' across the internet is an influencial, former Youtuber and creator of Game Theory.

Why MatPat for President?

MatPat has been a large advocate for change and bettering a generation. He has not only strived to create a platform where people feel safe and comfortable to be themselves but has encouraged curiosity and thought with his videos. He tries his best to be sympathetic and fair to people's issues regardless of their background, more interested in helping people rather than dividing people based off of their backgrounds, interests, biases, etc.

Markiplier for President

Similar to MatPat, Markiplier has also worked to make Youtube and the Creator space better for content creators and the community as a whole, encouraging creativity and self expression. While MatPat is more empathetic and kind to his audience, Markiplier is very straightforward and doesn't sugar coat anything. While these two have similar goals, their delivery and presentation is different.
