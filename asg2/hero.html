Fone Bone

An Unlikely Hero

Overview

Fone Bone from the 1991 comic series "Bone" is one of the main three characters featured within the story. He shares his adventure with his cousins Smiley
Bone and Phoney Bone. Considering his childlike nature, being optimistic, naive, and too trustworthy, it feels unlikely that someone like him
would become a hero, let alone someone who would save the Valley from total destruction. He does hold some heroic traits, being someone who is
loyal to his friends and family and strong conviction towards doing what he believes is right and helps those in need. He doesn't hesitate to

aid those in need even if it requires him to put himself in harms way or even risk his own life.
The perspective of the story switches between the three but focuses mainly on Fone Bone's story that he shares with Thorn.
Fone Bone's story starts off with his cousins. The three of them are stranded in the desert after being kicked out of the city Boneville after
Phoney Bone's plan to scam the citizens out of their money during his campaign to become mayor failed. The three of them find a map and are
chased off of a cliff by a swarm of locusts. From here, the three have entered the Valley with Fone Bone being separated from his cousins.
While in the Valley, Fone Bone is introduced to many of the important characters that will help him through his journey alongside common
antagonists, some that will be later redeemed and others that will prove to be stubborn foes. The story itself starts off as charming and
unasuming. It's not until Thorn has a nightmare that turns out to be a repressed memory of her past is the true plot revealed.
