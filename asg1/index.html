Semester 1 Project

For my semester 1 project I was torn between doing an aggregator of bakeries with their listed food allergies and a website that had all sorts of artist profiles compiled into one site.
The latter seemed to be more feasible so I have decided to make a website that compiles profiles of all types of online artists.
Just to name a few, this could be 2D digital illustrators, Background Artists, 3D modelers, Voice Actors, Sound Designers.
